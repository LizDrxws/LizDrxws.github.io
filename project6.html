Book Cover

Tool(s): Adobe Photoshop, ChatGPT, Firefly

Project Description: For the book cover, my partner and I decided to go with a thriller type novel and we typed in the title, subtitle, and the authro for the book in Chat GPT. Then I went to Firefly to try to generate an illustation for the book cover and after I had everything I needed I went to Photoshop and added the text to wrap it all together.

Project caption: Twisted Reality "When the truth becomes a mirage." by Rachel Nightshade
