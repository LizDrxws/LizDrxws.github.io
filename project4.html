12 months of fall

Tool(s): CapCut, Adobe Free Stock Footage, MP3 Downloader

Project Description: This project was orginally suppose to be made in premiere pro, but it wasn't running well on my computer so I used CapCut instead. To find the clips I wanted I went to Adobe free stock footage to get the fall clips. I also added the music in the background by copying the youtube link into an mp3 downloader to be able to place the music there.
